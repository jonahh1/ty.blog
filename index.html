Why I Decided To Apply Here

I have an interest in programming, I particularly like developing desktop apps in c#.
(ie. boring stuff like text editors or digital notepads)

The latest thing I am making is a Terminal or Shell but with a file explorer on the side to make it easier to visualise where you are when using a terminal.
Although I like making desktop apps I still don't know what I want to do once I leave school and thought this work experience program could help me decide, or at least give me a better understanding of what to expect from a job developing software. So far (Tuesday) I have enjoyed RedHat and I look forward to the rest of the week

Monday

Monday was an introduction to RedHat as a company. We were given an explanation of what "open source software" means and how RedHat manages to make money while being an open source company.
Before that however we were each assigned a laptop and told how to set it up with software we needed for the week. We were given a tour of the building and towards the end we set up the website/blog that you are reading right now

Tuesday

On Tuesday we were given an hour or so to work on our blog, I used the time to finalise the design I wanted to go for. There was a talk in the board room about what Agile is and how it helps developers to create and implement features quicker.

By then It was getting close to the break but after that there was a workshop on python and the micro:bit. After that had finished up we were given the chance to ask a few questions.

Wednesday

Today was a more relaxed day. There were two talks and in between them we had the task of building the "LEGO McLaren Formula 1 Race Car". One talk was about the life of a software engineer and the other was about what to expect from colleges or universities
